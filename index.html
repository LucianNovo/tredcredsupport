What is TredCred?

TredCred is an app that connects your treadmill workouts to Strava!

How does photo upload work?

You can upload photos from your workouts, and they will be shared with your Strava profile.

What is Strava Sync?

Strava Sync allows you to automatically share your treadmill workouts on Strava right after completion.

Your privacy is protected!

Your photos are never stored on our servers. TredCred guarantees that your data remains private.

What should I do if I have issues?

If you encounter any problems, please check our troubleshooting guide or contact support.

What features does TredCred offer?

TredCred offers various features including workout tracking, progress monitoring, and workout sharing.
