TredCred FAQ

1. How to Sync Your Treadmill to Strava?

To sync your treadmill workouts to Strava, connect your TredCred account to Strava under settings. Once connected, workouts will be automatically uploaded.

2. Can I Upload Photos?

Yes! You can upload photos of your workouts. Simply navigate to the upload section, and you can add photos to your workouts.

3. Privacy Guarantee

We guarantee that your photos are not stored on our servers. All uploads are processed locally and deleted after use.

4. Troubleshooting Information

If you are experiencing issues, please check the following:

  • Ensure your treadmill is connected to the internet.
  • Update the TredCred app to the latest version.
  • Restart your treadmill and try syncing again.
