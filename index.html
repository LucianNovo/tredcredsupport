Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the TredCred treadmill-to-Strava app?

The TredCred app allows users to seamlessly track their treadmill workouts and sync them to their Strava account, enhancing their fitness tracking experience.

2. How do I connect my Strava account to TredCred?

To connect your Strava account, navigate to the settings page in the app, select "Connect to Strava," and follow the authorization steps.

3. Can I manually enter my workouts?

Yes, you can manually log your treadmill workouts within the app if you prefer not to use automatic tracking.

4. What data does TredCred sync with Strava?

The app syncs your distance, time, pace, and calories burned from your treadmill workouts to Strava.

5. Is there a mobile version of TredCred?

Yes, TredCred is available as a mobile application for both iOS and Android devices.

6. Who can I contact for support?

If you have any issues or questions, please reach out to our support team via the support section in the app or email us at support@tredcred.com.

7. How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We welcome feedback! You can submit your suggestions through the feedback form available in the app.
